Retail Inventory & Supply Chain Analysis

Most retail stockouts begin long before a shelf becomes empty. Using PostgreSQL and Power BI, I analyzed inventory and supply chain operations across five store locations to identify the hidden drivers of stockouts, waste, and supplier inefficiencies. The project uncovered a 38% stockout rate occurring even during low demand periods, revealing that replenishment decisions, not customer demand, were the primary issue. The final solution included an executive dashboard and actionable recommendations across seven key operational areas.

About

I am a data analyst with a Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the University of Ibadan. My work sits at the intersection of analytics and operations. I look at business problems through data, measure what actually matters, and build tools that help people make better decisions.

I work regularly with SQL, Python, and Power BI. My projects have covered inventory planning, demand forecasting, route optimisation, and customer retention. I am particularly drawn to supply chain analytics, where small changes in forecasting and planning can have a large operational impact.

I approach each project the same way: understand the problem, get the data, analyse it thoroughly, and present findings clearly enough that someone can act on them.
